Products

Island Dressing

Island Dressings are sterile, self-adhesive, Non-woven wound dressings incorporating an Absorbent, non-adherent pad. Island Dressings feature a latex free Nonwoven fabric which is coated with a hypoallergenic acrylic adhesive. The dressing is breathable.

Hydrogel Dressing

Hydrogel dressing is a moisture-rich wound covering that maintains hydration, promotes healing, facilitates autolytic debridement, and provides pain relief through its cooling effect, making it ideal for dry or necrotic wounds. It is not suitable for heavily exuding or infected wounds as it may retain excess moisture.

Paraffin Gauze Dressing

Paraffin dressing is a sterile, open-weave gauze impregnated with soft paraffin that soothes and protects wounds, preventing adherence and minimizing trauma upon removal. It is often used with an absorbent secondary dressing to manage wound exudate effectively.

Calcium Alginate Dressing

Silver Calcium Alginate Dressings and Ropes are soft, Conformable wound dressing impregnated with Antimicrobial Silver. The silver- calcium alginate fibres, when in contact with wound exudate or blood, form a impregnated gel which creates a, moist wound healing environment.
